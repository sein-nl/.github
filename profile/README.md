**SEIN** is a Dutch center of expertise for **epilepsy and sleep medicine**. We are dedicated to improving the quality of life for people with epilepsy through an approach that combines specialized clinical care, long-term residential support and special-needs schooling. By combining routine care develivery with reseach, we aim to continously improve healthcare for people suffering from epilepsie.

## What can you find on GitHub
GitHub contains our the codebase for our internal Data Science Center (DSC) and our Business Intelligence Competence Center (BICC). We try to share as much as possible, in line with our commitment to contribute to open, reproducible science and the development of digital tools that support the global epilepsy community. Our focus areas include:

* **Automated Detection:** Developing machine learning models and algorithms for real-time seizure detection and event alerting (falls, apnea).
* **Neuroimaging & Signal Processing:** Tools for processing iEEG, DTI, and super-resolution imaging to assist in surgical planning and diagnosis.
* **Mobile Health (mHealth):** Leveraging smartphone data and touchscreen dynamics to monitor the neurobehavioral impact of epilepsy in real-world settings.
* **Data Standardization:** Contributing to frameworks like BIDS (Brain Imaging Data Structure) and OHDSI OMOP to ensure medical data is reproducible and accessible.
